Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21 February 2026

WatchTracker ProMic does not require user accounts and does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data.

Data Storage

All watch data, timing measurements, collection details, and service records are stored locally on the user's device. The app does not transmit this data to external servers.

Data Collection

WatchTracker ProMic does not collect personal information, usage analytics, location data, or tracking data.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks.

Data Sharing

No user data is shared with third parties.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact: watchtracker Privacy Policy – WatchTracker ProMic
